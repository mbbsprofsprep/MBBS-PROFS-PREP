Welcome to MBBS Profs Prep, India's premier digital learning portal dedicated to medical undergraduates.

Our Mission

Medical education is vast, demanding, and rigorous. Our mission is to simplify exam preparation for MBBS students across AIIMS and other leading medical colleges in India by providing meticulously curated, high-yield Previous Year Questions (PYQs) structured specifically for university professional exams.

What We Do

We aggregate, digitize, and systematically categorize academic data ranging from First Prof to Final Prof. Our platform allows students to filter questions by unit, chapter, and exam batch, saving countless hours of manual searching. Through our native app-like experience, students can bookmark tough questions, toggle MCQ answers, and review topics seamlessly on any device.

Our Vision

We aim to be the ultimate companion for every medical student in India, ensuring that no student walks into an exam hall unprepared. By bridging the gap between vast textbook curriculum and targeted exam patterns, we help shape confident, knowledgeable future doctors.
